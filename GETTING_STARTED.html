7
Set Up Cloud Backup (Free)

Your data auto-syncs to the cloud, encrypted with your passphrase. Follow CLOUD_BACKUP_SETUP.md to set up a free Cloudflare Worker in 5 minutes.

Data changes→ Encrypted on phone→ Pushed to cloud→ 30 daily snapshots

Once configured, go to Settings → ☁️ Cloud Backup, enter your Worker URL and passphrase, tap Save, then ☁️ Backup Now to test.

☁️ Cloud Backup
Synced 2m ago
Your passphrase never leaves your phone. The server only ever sees encrypted data. Write your passphrase down somewhere safe — if you lose it, backups can't be recovered.
8
Manual Export (Belt + Suspenders)

Tap More → 💾 Export & Backup. This downloads a JSON file with all your trips, expenses, fuel, and settings — protected by a SHA-256 checksum.

Tap Export→ JSON downloads→ Save to iCloud / Drive / Email
Do this weekly. Your data only lives on this device. If you lose your phone or clear browser data, it's gone unless you have a backup.

To restore: More → 📥 Import Data → pick your JSON backup file.

You can also export trips, expenses, or fuel as CSV for spreadsheets, or generate a Weekly Report image and an Accountant Package.

8
Snap Load — Scan Rate Confirmations

Tap ＋ → 📸 Snap Load. Take a photo of a rate confirmation and the app uses OCR to extract order number, broker, origin, destination, pay, and miles — then pre-fills a trip for you.

📸 Photo→ 🔍 OCR reads text→ 📝 Trip pre-filled→ ✓ Verify & save
Snap Load requires internet the first time (to download the OCR engine). After that it works offline if you've placed the vendor files locally.
9
Settings — Configure Your Profile

Tap More → scroll down to Tax & Reports → scroll to Settings.

Key settings to configure:

  • Weekly gross goal — Sets your target (e.g., $4,200). Drives the coaching system.
  • Vehicle MPG — Your truck's fuel economy. Used for fuel cost estimates.
  • Fuel price — Set slightly above actual as a buffer.
  • Home Location — e.g., "Indianapolis, IN". Loads going home auto-enable the $1.25 strategic floor.
  • Operating cost per mile — Insurance, truck payment, maintenance, tires — everything except fuel, divided by monthly miles. This unlocks true profit analysis.
  • UI Mode — Set to Pro to enable trip deletion and hard reset.
Operating cost per mile is the single most impactful setting. Without it, the evaluator can only estimate fuel-based margins. With it, you get true profit analysis.

USA Engine Modes
🟢 Harvest — You're in density. Tightest RPM floor ($1.60). Maximize yield.
🔵 Reposition — Outside target density. Lower floor ($1.45) if destination is anchor/support.
🟡 Escape — Stuck in bad zone. Lowest floor ($1.40). Prioritize exiting.
🟠 Floor Protect — Late-week. Moderate floor ($1.50). Favor certainty.
